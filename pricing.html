Frequently Asked Questions

Why is NestQuest free?

We believe every parent deserves access to great parenting tools. Our core mission is to support families, and we're committed to keeping essential features free forever.

Will there be ads?

No. We will never show ads or sell your data. Premium features will be our only revenue source, ensuring we stay aligned with your privacy.

What about my data?

Your data stays on your device. We use peer-to-peer sync between caregivers - no cloud storage, no data mining, complete privacy.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely! When premium features launch, you'll be able to upgrade seamlessly while keeping all your existing data.
