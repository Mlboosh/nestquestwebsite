Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NestQuest and how it works.

Getting Started

What is NestQuest?

NestQuest is a privacy-first baby tracking app that gamifies the parenting experience. Track feeds, nappies, sleep, play, health, and developmental milestones while earning achievements and leveling up through your parenting journey.

How do I get started?

Simply download the app from the App Store and start tracking immediately. No account creation, no email signup - just open the app and begin your NestQuest journey.

Is NestQuest really free?

Yes! All core features are free forever. We believe every parent deserves access to great parenting tools. Premium features may be added in the future, but the essential tracking and gamification will always be free.

Privacy & Security

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. We don't use cloud servers or external databases - your family's information never leaves your control.

How does multi-caregiver sync work?

We use peer-to-peer sync via Bluetooth, NFC, and local WiFi. Caregivers can pair their devices and sync data directly without any cloud servers. All sync data is encrypted end-to-end.

Do you collect any personal information?

No. We don't collect emails, names, or any personal information. We don't use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking. We literally don't know who our users are or how they use the app.

What happens if I lose my phone?

If you have another paired device, your data is safe there. You can also enable automatic backups to iCloud or Google Drive (optional). We also provide export features so you can regularly backup your data.

Features

What activities can I track?

Track feeds, nappy changes, sleep, play time, crying episodes, health checkups, weight measurements, skin-to-skin contact, and expressing/pumping sessions. Each activity has beautiful animated icons and detailed logging options.

How does the gamification work?

Earn XP for logging activities, complete daily quests, unlock achievements for both baby progress and parenting milestones. Watch your baby progress through an interactive skill tree as they reach developmental milestones.

Can I track multiple babies?

Yes! Create unlimited baby profiles. Each baby has their own tracking, achievements, and skill tree. Perfect for twins, siblings, or childcare providers managing multiple children.

What are parent mood tracking and collaborative todos?

Track your emotional wellness with 8 distinct mood states using beautiful video-based selection. Manage tasks with smart assignment, XP rewards, and real-time sync between partners with "Me", "Open", and specific assignment options.

Technical

Which devices are supported?

Currently available for iOS devices. Android version is coming soon. The app works on iPhone and iPad with iOS 14.0 or later.

Does NestQuest work offline?

Yes! NestQuest works 100% offline. All features, tracking, and insights are generated locally on your device. Internet is only needed for initial app download and updates.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely! Export all your data anytime in standard formats (CSV, JSON, PDF reports). Your data is yours - take it anywhere, share it with healthcare providers, or keep personal backups.

How do app updates work?

Updates are delivered through the App Store. Your local data is preserved during updates. We're committed to maintaining backward compatibility so your historical data is never lost.
