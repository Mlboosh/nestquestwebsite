Privacy Policy

Complete transparency about data handling practices. NestQuest's privacy-first architecture ensures user data never leaves their control.

Privacy Overview

Key privacy principles and data handling practices that protect user families and ensure complete data sovereignty.

Data Protection

Zero data collection policy with local-only storage. User family information remains exclusively on their devices under their complete control.

No Tracking

No analytics, crash reporting, or usage statistics collection. The development team has no visibility into user behavior or app usage patterns.

Secure Storage

Encrypted local databases with no cloud dependencies. All data processing occurs on-device with industry-standard security protocols.

Transparency

Complete openness about data practices with clear, understandable privacy policies. No hidden data collection or undisclosed third-party integrations.

User Rights

Complete data ownership and control rights. Users can export, delete, or transfer their data at any time without restrictions or penalties.

Compliance

Full compliance with GDPR, COPPA, and international privacy regulations. Privacy-by-design architecture exceeds regulatory requirements.
