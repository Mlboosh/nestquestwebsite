Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

The Simple Version

We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Period.

NestQuest is built with privacy-first architecture. All your family's data stays on your device, and we have no way to access it.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike most apps, NestQuest is designed to collect zero personal information:

  • No email addresses or account information
  • No names, photos, or personal details
  • No baby data, tracking information, or logs
  • No usage analytics or app behavior data
  • No device identifiers or location data
  • No crash reports or diagnostic information

2. How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into NestQuest is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. This includes:

  • Baby profiles and information
  • Activity logs (feeds, sleep, nappies, etc.)
  • Milestone and achievement data
  • Parent mood tracking
  • Todo lists and collaborative tasks
  • App settings and preferences

3. Data Sharing Between Caregivers

When you sync data with other caregivers:

  • Data is transferred directly between devices using peer-to-peer technology
  • All transfers are encrypted end-to-end
  • No data passes through our servers or third-party services
  • You control who can access your family's information

4. Third-Party Services

NestQuest does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services (unless you explicitly choose to backup)

5. Data Backup and Export

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export all data anytime in standard formats
  • Optional backup to your personal iCloud or Google Drive
  • Delete all data permanently from the app settings
  • No data retention on our end when you delete the app

6. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously:

  • No data about children is collected or transmitted
  • All baby information stays on your device
  • Compliant with COPPA and GDPR regulations
  • No advertising or marketing to children

7. Contact Information

If you contact us for support:

  • We only use your contact information to respond to your inquiry
  • We don't add you to mailing lists without explicit consent
  • Support conversations are deleted after resolution
  • No personal information is shared with third parties

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever need to update this privacy policy:

  • We'll notify users through the app
  • Changes will be posted on our website
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • We'll never change our core privacy principles

9. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU:

  • We process no personal data, so GDPR largely doesn't apply
  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • You have complete control and ownership of your data
  • No consent needed as we don't collect personal information

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

  • Email: privacy@nestquest.app
  • Contact form: nestquest.app/contact

Our Commitment: We built NestQuest because we believe parents deserve privacy. This policy reflects our core values and technical architecture - we literally cannot access your data even if we wanted to.
